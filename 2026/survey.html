About This Survey

This comprehensive survey aims to create the most detailed picture of Zimbabwe's tech ecosystem. Following the models of Stack Overflow's annual Developer Survey and OfferZen's State of the Nation, we're gathering data to help:

For Developers:
  • Benchmark salaries and compensation
  • Understand career progression paths
  • Identify skill gaps and learning opportunities
  • Connect with community resources
For Companies & Policy Makers:
  • Understand hiring market trends
  • Improve workplace policies
  • Shape education and training programs
  • Inform tech industry regulations
üìä Comprehensive Data Collection: This survey covers demographics, compensation, tech stack, work environment, career development, challenges, and future outlook.
